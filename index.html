Self-Signed Certificate Notice

Your browser will show a security warning. This is expected for self-signed certificates. Click "Advanced" → "Proceed" to continue.

Quick Guide

  1. 1 Enter your server's IP address or hostname
  2. 2 Keep the default port (6443) unless configured otherwise
  3. 3 Click "Connect to Desktop" to launch the VNC viewer
  4. 4 Accept the certificate warning (first time only)
  5. 5 Enter your VNC password when prompted

Troubleshooting

Connection refused?

Ensure the noVNC service is running and port 6443 is open

Certificate error?

First visit https://[server]:6443 directly to accept the cert

Black screen?

The desktop environment may need a moment to start
